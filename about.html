OUR FOUNDER

MR. SALEEM

(FOUNDER)

With hard work, dedication and a clear vision, Mr. Saleem laid the foundation of SS Traders in 1982. His relentless efforts, deep industry knowledge and honest approach have been the driving force behind the success of the company for more than four decades. Today, SS Traders is recognized as one of the trusted names in used heavy machinery and genuine spare parts, serving customers with quality products, transparency and dependable after-sales support.
